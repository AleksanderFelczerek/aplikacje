<h2>The style Attribute</h2>
<p>The style attribute is used to specify the styling of an element, like color:</p>

<p style="color:red">This is a paragraph.</p>

<p style="color:#00FF00">This is a paragraph.</p>

<p style="color:#0000FF">This is a paragraph.</p>

<p style="color:#FFFF00">This is a paragraph.</p>

<p style="color:#00FFFF">This is a paragraph.</p>

<p style="color:#FF00FF">This is a paragraph.</p>

<p style="color:#CCCCCC">This is a paragraph.</p>

<p style="color:#000000">This is a paragraph.</p>
